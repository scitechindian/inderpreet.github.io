About Inderpreet Singh

I design things, I make them, I reinvent them and teach them. #DIY, #research, #IoT, #embedded, #RaspberryPi, #Arduino, #EEE.
This is a single page portfolio for project work executed under the Open Source License. My blog is available at The Embedded Code and if you are a student, DIY guy/gal or someone who needs help with a project, please visit the blog and if you can't find what you are looking for, contact me and we will see what we can do for you.

The more popular projects I have done are documented at my blog and the list of projects and links to the project pages are given below.

In case you want me to review your product or have a project in mind that you would like my help or you need a demo project on your product, I request you to contact me on twitter since it is the quickest way for me to respond. Alternatively, there is a contact segment on my blog which allows you to send me an email and I will get back to you when I can.

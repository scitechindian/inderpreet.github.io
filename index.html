About Inderpreet Singh

I design things, I make them, I reinvent them and teach them. #DIY, #research, #IoT, #embedded, #RaspberryPi, #Arduino, #EEE.
I have been working with electronics for over two decades and have helped establish research labs and products for industrial automation in India. I have a passion to create something new and improve the existing world and technology is my tool. I offer the following commercial services.

Product Design: From Idea to concept to production, I can help you realize your dream project.

Growth Hacking: Use the in ternet to your advantage. I can help you get to your target audience.

Training: Add skills to your organization. I can help introduce new technology and workflows to your existing team.

My blog is available at The Embedded Code

In case you want me to review your product or have a project in mind that you would like my help or you need a demo project on your product, I request you to contact me on twitter since it is the quickest way for me to respond. Alternatively, there is a contact segment on my blog which allows you to send me an email and I will get back to you when I can.

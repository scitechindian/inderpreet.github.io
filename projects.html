Smart Container

A design for perishable goods transport.

One of the problems when transporting perishable goods on ship over long distances is that preservation systems may fail. It is important to have a monitoring system to keep an eye on such good and generate alerts when something goes wrong. We built a smart container which had all the sensors and power systems contained within the walls of the container itself so that no tampering was possible. We use the Wurth Electronics Wireless Power system to recharge the system and make everthing work.
